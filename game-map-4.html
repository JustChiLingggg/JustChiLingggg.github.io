Find The Signatures!

Game Information:

In Find The Signatures!,

You are tasked with finding 1 of Toast Box's signatures in each of the 4 provided maps.

Good luck!

Map Fun Fact!

This map is a picture of the store concept "HDB 1970" for Toast Box from 2016 to present.

This store concept is based on the novelty of HDBs during the 1960s to 70s, where kampongs transitioned into urbanized communal spaces.
