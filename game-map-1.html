Find The Signatures!

Game Information:

In Find The Signatures!,

You are tasked with finding 1 of Toast Box's signatures in each of the 4 provided maps.

Good luck!

Map Fun Fact!

This map is a picture of the store concept "Old Nanyang" for Toast Box from 2005 to 2007.

It is based on Singapore in the 1950s to 60s with a traditional coffee house setting enlivened with Nanyang vintage display reminiscent of yesteryears.

Text obtained from Toast Box Story.
