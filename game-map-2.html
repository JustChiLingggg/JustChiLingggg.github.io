Find The Signatures!

Game Information:

In Find The Signatures!,

You are tasked with finding 1 of Toast Box's signatures in each of the 4 provided maps.

Good luck!

Map Fun Fact!

This map is a picture of the store concept "British Colonial" for Toast Box from 2008 to 2009.

It is based on their version of the British colonial style that imparts a nostalgic and relaxing ambience for customers to enjoy their meals.

Text obtained from Toast Box Story.
