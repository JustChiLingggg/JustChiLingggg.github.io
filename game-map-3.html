Find The Signatures!

Game Information:

In Find The Signatures!,

You are tasked with finding 1 of Toast Box's signatures in each of the 4 provided maps.

Good luck!

Map Fun Fact!

This map is a picture of the store concept "Modern Rustic" for Toast Box from 2010 to 2015.

This is a space centred around being contemporary with refreshing rustic touches to relax around nature while not getting baked by the sun.
